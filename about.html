About AVAV Yogic Traits

AVAV is an integrative model combining ancient yogic principles with modern scientific evaluation. Our comprehensive system measures 40 parameters across four categories—Aahara (Diet & Nutrition), Vihara (Lifestyle & Daily Routine), Achara (Conduct, Behavior, and Ethics), and Vichara (Thought Process & Mental Discipline)—using a weighted scoring system for precise, personalized insights. Inspired by the "Temple of Nature," our approach emphasizes a deep connection with the natural world.

Explore our calculator to receive a detailed analysis of your traits, track improvements over time, and download reports for future reference.
